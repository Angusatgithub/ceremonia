About us
honouring life’s significant events

Ceremonia is all about honouring life’s significant events, and enhancing the occasion through a celebration. Whether it is a wedding, milestone birthday, wedding anniversary, renewal of vows, a naming ceremony, or a significant achievement, I will curate a ceremony that will capture the significance of the event and create a beautiful memory.

Lovingly curated ceremonies

Ceremonia will curate the perfect ceremony. I am committed to you and your needs and desires. I will work with you to create a memorable ceremony that will bring smiles or laughs while acknowledging the significance of the occasion. I will provide as much or as little guidance as you require to ensure your confidence in the ceremony. I will take you, step by step, through the process. I will provide a ceremony that is relaxed but thoughtful, that reflects your values and who you are, always respectful of all your wishes.

Creating beautiful ceremonies is a delightful task to be charged with, be it for a wedding or other significant event. I take exceptional pride in this work and my ceremonies reflect that. Your ceremony will be personal and unique to you. Your celebration is all about you and I’m here to make it happen for you.
